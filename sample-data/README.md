# Sample Bank Statements

This folder contains sample bank statements for testing the CFOSync application.

## Individual User Statements

### 1. `bank_statement_checking.csv`
A monthly checking account statement with:
- Income from salary deposits
- Regular expenses (rent, groceries, utilities, entertainment)
- Transfers to savings and investments
- Various transaction categories

### 2. `bank_statement_savings.csv`
A savings account statement showing:
- Interest earnings
- Transfers from checking
- Credit card payments

### 3. `credit_card_statement.csv`
A credit card statement with:
- Various purchase categories
- Payment transactions
- Subscription services

### 4. `bank_statement_formatted.txt`
A formatted bank statement document (PDF-like text format) with:
- Account summary
- Detailed transactions
- Spending breakdown by category
- Income summary

## Company/Business Statement

### 5. `company_bank_statement.csv`
A business bank statement with:
- Customer payments (revenue)
- Payroll expenses
- Software subscriptions
- Marketing spend
- Professional services
- Office expenses

## Usage

1. Upload these files in the Individual or Company onboarding flow
2. The AI will parse and categorize transactions automatically
3. Use for testing financial insights and analysis features

## Categories Included

**Individual:**
- Income, Groceries, Dining, Transportation, Entertainment
- Housing, Utilities, Shopping, Health, Insurance, Pets

**Company:**
- Revenue, Payroll, Benefits, Software, Rent, Marketing
- Contractors, Professional Services, Equipment, Utilities
